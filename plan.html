Beach Volleyball Website

Written by Jason Paix - 12839334

Goals

The purpose of this website is to increase the number of customers playing at Slammers Beach Volleyball on a weekly basis.

This website will provide more publicity for the company and attract new customers who might like to play.

Success Evaluation

It is expected that this website will increase the public's interest in playing volleyball at Slammers Beach Volleyball, thus increasing the number of customers. Expectations of this website is to have an increase of customers of at least 50%, whilst also increasing the number of younger players aged 15-30 years old.

Target Audience

Primarily the site is desired to appeal to a younger audience, preferably attracting kids to play while they are in high school. Therefore, the main target audience is teenagers and young adults aged 15-30; however the website should also not repulse older potential customers.
